Move Management Services

PAX FREIGHT is your one source for all your worldwide moving services. Whether it’s household goods, family pets, professional equipment’s or personal vehicles, we have the resources, expertise and global coverage, to plan and co-ordinate all the aspects of your moving requirements. After all, PAX FREIGHT’s worldwide network and this ensures total security and peace of mind at all times.

From start to finish, you will receive professional, courteous and trouble-free service. And throughout this process, one of our highly-skilled Removal Supervisor and Move Manager will carefully manage and coordinate your entire move be it household goods, vehicles, pets or all of together every step of the way.

PAX FREIGHT – Multiple Locations. One Company. One Contact.

Management

At PAX FREIGHT, selection of our staff is critical. Whether it’s an apprentice packer or graduate trainee, our professional staff benefit from training in practical packing skills, languages, customs and shipping procedures and most importantly, to consistently recognize that the customer always comes first.

Your move will be handled by a friendly and knowledgeable coordinator who is your personal point of contact. Your coordinator, through our computerized tracking systems, will know where your shipment is at all times and communicate on its status at each stage.

Packing and Crating

When you move with PAX FREIGHT you can be sure that your valuable possessions will be carefully handled and protected to the highest standards in this industry. PAX FREIGHT uses high quality material, such as padded multi-layer furniture wraps and glass and china are packed using the clean-pack system before being placed into especially reinforced export cartons which are regularly tested to comply with world standards. In line with our commitment to a quality environment, all paper materials are recyclable.

Family treasures are identified and professional advice given as to the most secure method of shipment – a caring approach to your most valued possessions. Work of arts and antiques receive special attention and can be packed into custom built crates to ensure optimum protection during their journey. Silverware is wrapped in acid free tissue paper to prevent tarnishing particularly when moving to hotter and humid climates. Clothing can either be placed on hanging rails in strong wardrobe cartons or laid flat in suitcase cartons.

Worldwide Moving by Sea, Road and Air

PAX FREIGHT understands the importance of meeting your deadlines. When moving overseas, our in-house freight forwarding department will expertly guide your shipment and coordinate delivery through one of our carefully selected carriers safely and quickly, door to door anywhere in the world.

The intricacies of customs procedures and local regulations need never be a problem. PAX FREIGHT’s vast experience in diplomatic and executive worldwide moving ensures smooth dealings with customs authorities worldwide.
